Welcome to your loyalty program control room!

Here you can customize the appearance of your loyalty stamp card, manage customer rewards, and download your store-specific checkout counter QR code.

Getting Started is Simple:

  1. Go to the Branding & Card tab to customize colors, choose a stamp icon, and set your rewards description.
  2. Visit the QR Code Link tab, download your QR code, and print it at your register.
  3. When customers scan it, they can register in seconds under your cafe's brand scope.
  4. Use مسح الزبون to scan each customer's personal QR — one stamp per scan.
  5. Or go to Customers to adjust stamps manually.
